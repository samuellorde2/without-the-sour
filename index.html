MEET RYAN V

Ryan V is a MENTAL HEALTH REBEL and podcast host from Canada now living in Aotearoa (New Zealand). With over 25 years of personal ADHD experience, Ryan gets the raw reality of living with neurodiversity. After his own brutal journey through ADHD burnout, Ryan went full REBEL MODE to create this platform as a trusted friend you can talk to about ADHD burnout and mental health.
